Why the length of the blog post is important:

Choosing a maximum blog post length comes with the many benefits of your blog and your website. One of the main reasons for starting blogs is that Google's algorithm is to improve its website and improve their performance on search engines. This process is called SEO, or search engine correction. When it comes to SEO, marketers focus on keyword research and backlinks, but the length of the content can also play a big role in signaling Google that your blog is better than the post competition. Although SEO is an important part of blogging, a great blog will take into account its readers. Users also care about the length of the blog post. Each industry is different, so "How long should the blog post be?" The answer may be different for you.
